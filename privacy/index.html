Privacy Policy

XCSB Player (“the App”) is developed by Sonic Eye Industries.

Overview

The App provides access to the official XCSB online radio stream and related program information. It functions as a client for streaming audio content operated by XCSB.

Information Handling

The App does not require user accounts or registration.

The App does not store personal data or listening history on the user’s device.

The App may transmit an anonymous listener signal to XCSB for the purpose of calculating total concurrent listener counts. This signal does not include personally identifiable information and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Streaming Content

All audio content is streamed directly from XCSB’s servers. Sonic Eye Industries does not host, store, or modify any audio content.

XCSB is responsible for all broadcast programming, content management, and licensing.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or cross-app tracking.

Data Sharing

Sonic Eye Industries does not sell personal data.

Any data collected by XCSB, including aggregated listener counts, is governed by XCSB’s own policies.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the App or on this page.
